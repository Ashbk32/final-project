I used can chickpeas but you can do fresh but just soak them for the night or let them sit in boiling water for 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • One can of chickpeas, save the pea water
  • Sprouts for topping, you can use broccoli sprouts or alfalfa sprouts your choice
  • 1 lemon
  • 1 chopped onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • pepper
  • Olive oil or any oil of choice
  • Fresh cut up fruit. I used apple, pineapple

Pancake

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Drop of vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Almond/oat/soy/hemp milk
  • Vanilla

Instructions

  1. Chop your onions, garlic and added to a heated pan with oil to coat the pan. Precook you chick peas by placing them in salty boiling water, let cook until tender
  2. Let the onions heat up and then add your chickpeas, Squeeze lemon, add in you salt & pepper.
  3. Taste to make sure seasoned well then put to the side
  4. For pancake, in a bowl put together flour, salt, baking powder, sugar. Mix together.
  5. In another bowl add the milk of choice, vinegar, vanilla, stir that up. Pour wet into dry and mix but not too well, let sit for 5 minutes so the batter can develop.
  6. On a hot skillet sprayed down with any oil pour your pancakes into the skillet. Once bubbles form on top it's time for flipping. Cook until golden brown.
  7. Assembly plate.
  8. Hot cakes first, add your fruit on top or the side drizzle with Maple syrup.
  9. Add your chickpea on the side, place your sprouts on top.
